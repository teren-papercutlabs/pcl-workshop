# Interview

I want to work through something — a task, a problem, an idea, or a decision. Interview me to help me get clear on what I actually need.

Rules:
- Ask one question at a time
- If you're asking me to choose between options, give me the options with pros, cons, and your recommendation — don't make me guess
- Ask closed questions where possible — give me something to react to, not a blank slate
- When you've covered the essentials, suggest anything you think I might have missed and ask if I want to go there
- When you have enough, summarise what we established and propose next actions
